Analysis of
Pasik:

Optimizing Delivery Efficiency and Customer Satisfaction through Web-Based Logistics Automation

Pasik Logistics Automtion Project Overview:

Pasik, a private logistics company, specializes in delivering products for Ju e-commerce. Currently, Pasik relies on manual processes, receiving delivery requests via email round the clock with attached excel documents. These documents contain details such as the recipient's name, contact information and item pickup details. Recently, Pasik has faced increased customer complaints due to delayed deliveries. This is often caused by lost or overlooked delivery requests within the email-based system. Additionally, the current process lacks a mechanism for verifying successful deliveries. Consequently, the company has experienced reduced customer patronage and missed financial targets.

Proposed Solution:

Pasik is currently developing a web application to streamline the delivery process. This application will enable you to directly submit delivery requests, which will be automatically assigned to available Pasik dispatch riders. Upon receiving a delivery request notification on their mobile app, dispatch riders contact the beneficiary to confirm the pickup and delivery details. Once confirmed, they proceed with the pickup and deliver the item to the customer. Upon successful delivery, the customer verifies the delivery and provides a closure code, which the rider inputs into the app to complete the delivery process. The e-commerce team is then notified, and the system updates the delivery status to "completed."
