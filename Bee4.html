Analysis of
Bee4:

In-Store Congestion and Promoting Growth through E-Commerce Implementation

Bee4 E-commerce Project Overview:

Bee4 is a retail company involved in the sales of daily consumables and skin care products. They have been in existence for just a year. With the increasing customer base and the need to keep customers satisfied with their services, the management has frowned deeply at the inconveniences caused when a lot of customers are in the store. The management has decided to commence online shopping with both door step delivery and store pick-up as they cannot afford to buy/rent new store space. As a business Analyst what elicitation technique(s) will be used in this project?

Problem statement:

Bee4 company faced a growing customer base leading to in-store congestion, impacting customer satisfaction negatively.

Proposed Solution

Bee4 company will put in place an easy-to-use online shopping platform that clients can explore and buy their daily necessities and skincare goods from online using web browsers (and possibly a mobile-responsive design for future mobile app development).


As a Business Analyst the following Elicitation techniques were deployed to acquire the necessary information to address this challenge.

  1. Stakeholders such as management, customer service staff, and customers will be interview to understand their feelings and desire of the whole experience.
  2. Organize requirements Workshop, to collaborate with representatives from different departments and brainstorm features of proposed online platform.
  3. Document Analysis: This will assist to understand the current state, identify data points relevant to online sales and uncover any other exiting problem.
  4. Observation, taking time to observe customer inflow to understand the exact nature of congestion will be helpful
  5. Customer Surveys: If time and resources permits online survey with customers will be conducted to understand their interest in the online shopping.

A summarised BRD for the online shopping

Business Goals and Objectives:
The management frowned at the congestion causing the footfall in the store. Implementing online platform with delivery at door step will tackle this issue and create an alternative shopping channel.

Key Features

  • Product Catalog & Browsing
  • Search Functionality
  • Shopping Cart & Checkout
  • Secure Checkout Process
  • Shipping/Pick-Up Selection
  • Order and payment Confirmation
  • Doorstep Delivery

Functional Requirements:

Someone who has difficulty in travelling should be able to order for consumables and skin care online and have them delivered to him at the door steps, so that the products can access without facing the transportation challenges.

Customers who know exactly what they want can order online and just pick it up at the store, saving time to stay on long queues

Customers who are interested in skincare will have the opportunity to browse the products they want and check out the reviews and make appropriate decision.

Skills Demonstrated include:

  • Requirements Elicitation
  • Business Analysis Planning
  • Business Requirements Documentation
  • Problem-Solving
  • Solution Design
  • Understanding of E-commerce Principles
  • Process Modelling (for store pick-up)
